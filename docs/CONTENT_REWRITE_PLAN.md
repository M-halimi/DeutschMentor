## Content Quality Assessment

### What's Good
- **Lesson structure** is well-organized with clear CEFR progression in titles
- **Grammar topics** follow proper CEFR order (A1: articles/present → C2: nominal style/rhetoric)
- **Vocabulary** is mostly unique per level (84 cross-level words are pedagogical/academic, acceptable)
- **98% unique exercises**, **97% unique test questions** — template blast cleaned
- **All 237 lesson titles** are distinct and level-appropriate
- **Module themes** cover expected CEFR domains (ALL, ALL+ for B2-C2)

### What Needs Work

#### 1. Exercise/Test Quality (P1)
- **1 exercise** "Welcher Satz ist korrekt?" appears ×8 across levels — needs level-specific variants
- **1 test question** "Welcher Satz ist korrekt?" appears ×13 — same
- Remaining 1,126 exercises and 1,336 test questions exist but their content quality is unknown (need per-lesson review)

#### 2. Skill Coverage Gap (P2)
- A1 has 19 skill lessons (R+W+L+S). Upper levels have only 4-7 each.
- **A2**: only 5 skill lessons out of 36 (4 skill + 1 review + 1 test + rest vocab/grammar)
- **Reading**: 17 total (A1 has 9, others have 1-3 each)
- **Writing**: 10 total — critical gap for B1+ (exam prep)
- **Listening**: 11 total — too few for all levels
- **Speaking**: 9 total — insufficient for communicative competence

#### 3. A1 Top-Heaviness (P3)
- A1 has 57 lessons vs 36 for all other levels
- M2 (Familie und Alltag) has 12 lessons — nearly double other A1 modules
- Suggests A1 content could be consolidated to ~42 lessons

#### 4. Premium Content (P1)
- Premium tables (lesson_expressions, lesson_flashcards, etc.) exist but their content relationship to core lessons needs verification

## Rewrite Priority

### Phase 1: Fix Exercise/Test Quality (estimated 2-3 days)
- Replace "Welcher Satz ist korrekt?" with level-specific questions
- Verify exercise content is CEFR-aligned for each lesson
- Tasks:
  - A1: verify exercises use only A1 vocabulary
  - A2: verify exercises use only A1-A2 structures
  - B1+: verify exercises avoid A1-level content
  - Remove remaining template artifacts

### Phase 2: Expand Skill Coverage (estimated 5-7 days)
- Add reading, writing, listening, speaking lessons to A2-C2
- Target: minimum 3-4 skill lessons per module (not just 1)
- Priority order: Writing (for exam prep), Speaking, Listening, Reading
- Tasks:
  - Create 24 new writing lessons (3 per level for B1-C2, 2 for A2)
  - Create 18 new listening lessons (3 per level for B1-C2, 2 for A2)  
  - Create 18 new speaking lessons (3 per level for B1-C2, 2 for A2)
  - Create 12 new reading lessons (2 per level for B1-C2)

### Phase 3: Consolidate A1 (estimated 1 day)
- Merge M2 lessons where appropriate (12 → 9)
- Review overall A1 module balance

### Phase 4: Premium Content Mapping (estimated 2 days)
- Verify lesson_expressions, lesson_flashcards, lesson_conversations etc. are properly linked
- Fill gaps where lessons are missing premium content

## Total Estimated Effort: 10-13 days

## Recommendation
- **Start with Phase 1** (exercise/test quality) — quick wins, highest impact
- **Then Phase 2** (skill coverage) — largest effort, biggest user-facing improvement
- Phase 3-4 can be done in parallel or deferred
